Moroccan Arabic Dialect Electronic Dictionary (MADED) is an electronic lexicon containing almost 11.500 entries. They are written in Arabic script wherein each Moroccan Arabic dialect (MA) lemma is provided with its corresponding Moden Standard Arabic (MSA) equivalent. In addition, MADED entries are annotated with useful metadata such as part-of-speech (POS), root and origin (Arabic, French, ...). This dictionary is provided as an xml file and represents about 1 Mb of data
